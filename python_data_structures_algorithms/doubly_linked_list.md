# Doubly Linked List

```
head
  |
  v
+--------+      +--------+      +--------+      +--------+      +--------+
|   1    | <--> |   2    | <--> |   3    | <--> |   4    | <--> |   5    |
+--------+      +--------+      +--------+      +--------+      +--------+
                                                                    ^
                                                                    |
                                                                    tail
```
